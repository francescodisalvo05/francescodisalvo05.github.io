Black Lives Matter Sentiment Analysis

Sentiment analysis based on a collection of 80.000 tweets collected from 2020-05-31 to 2020-06-08 about the Black Lives Matter movement. This solution lays the foundation of Natural Language Processing by using some of the most common techniques, such as tokenization, stemming, stopwords elimination and n-grams implementation.

NYC Airbnb Price Prediction

Regression analysis on the New York City Airbnb Open Data, where the aim is to predict the price of a listing, based on fifteen parameters. In particular, the current approach is based on a specific domain analysis, in which it has been discovered the parameters that most affect the price value. So, once the data were cleaned, it was possible to accomplish the regression task.

Spoken Digit Classification

Classification analysis on the Free Spoken Digit Dataset, a collection of spoken digitis from 0 to 9. The proposed approach is based on both time and frequency domains, in order to gain insights about the length, amplitude and frequency. The Classification model was fed with the Mel-frequency cepstral coefficients derived from a cepstral representation of the audio track.
